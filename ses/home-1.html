Who We Are

We are a company committed to providing individualized employment services for people with barriers to employment. Southeastern Employment Services, LLC (SES) is made up of dedicated professionals with expertise in autism, developmental disabilities, and mental and physical health challenges. The services that are funded through numerous state and federal agencies are free for the person and the employer.

Our Roles & Ways of Being

  • A Professional
  • We maintain confidentiality and professional boundaries at all times and are dedicatedx to the highest level of professionalism, ethics, and integrity.
  • A Facilitator
  • We see ourselves as "dream facilitators" by expanding the range of opportunities and choices, while remaining hopeful and positive about what is possible.
  • A Coach
  • We understand that our primary responsibility is to provide coaching and support in a way that is positive, proactive, encouraging, comprehensive, and empowering; while meeting both the needs of the individual and the employer's expectations.
  • A Liaison
  • We communicate and cooperate in a way that facilitates close working relationships between all people involved in the employment process.
  • A Community Educator
  • We provide education and information when necessary, to expand awareness and knowledge to others about supporting people with barriers to employment.
  • A Team-Player
  • We believe in the power of working together, collaborating and utilizing the collective strengths of our employees and our partners.
  • We know that the way that we conduct ourselves as an employee, in each of these roles, has a direct impact on the reputation of our services and the individuals we support in employment.
