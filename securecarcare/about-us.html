About Us
(800) 989-8086

Secure Car Care is dedicated to protecting its customers from costly repairs after their auto warranties expire. Our goal is to build caring, long-term relationship with our customers.

We achieve this goal by providing the best fully-insured, extended vehicle service plans (more commonly referred to as “extended auto warranties”) with excellent customer service. And because we’ve earned our customers’ trust, they often purchase multiple vehicle service plans and refer the people they care about to us.

When you purchase one of our A.M. Best’s A Rated extended vehicle service plans, you are investing in more than just protection for your vehicle and your wallet. You get a dedicated team of professionals who are available to take care of your needs 24 / 7 starting from the time you request a quote . And they will be there if you need them should you break down on the side of the road or if you bring your vehicle to an ASE certified location.

Plus, as a proud member of the American Auto Assurance network, we believe in providing a transparent, informative, exemplary customer experience by adhering to the Vehicle Protection Association's standards.
